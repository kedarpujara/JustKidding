ACCOUNT DELETION REQUEST

JustKidding - Pediatric Telemedicine App

Last Updated: January 2026

HOW TO REQUEST ACCOUNT DELETION

If you wish to delete your JustKidding account and all associated data, you can do so using one of the following methods:

1. IN-APP DELETION (Recommended)
   - Open the JustKidding app
   - Navigate to Settings > Account
   - Tap "Delete Account"
   - Confirm your request
   - Your account will be scheduled for deletion

2. EMAIL REQUEST
   - Send an email to: [Your Support Email]
   - Subject: Account Deletion Request
   - Include the following information:
     * Registered phone number
     * Full name of the account holder
     * Reason for deletion (optional)
   - You will receive a confirmation within 48 hours

WHAT DATA WILL BE DELETED

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

- Guardian/Parent profile information (name, phone, email, address)
- Child/Patient profiles and information
- Profile pictures and uploaded files
- Appointment history
- Payment transaction records (excluding legally required records)
- Push notification tokens
- All associated preferences and settings

DATA THAT MAY BE RETAINED

In compliance with healthcare regulations and legal requirements, certain data may be retained:

- Medical consultation records and prescriptions (as required by healthcare regulations)
- Transaction records required for tax/accounting purposes
- Data required to comply with legal obligations

Retained data will be anonymized where possible and kept only for the minimum period required by law.

PROCESSING TIME

- In-app deletion: Processed within 7 days
- Email requests: Processed within 30 days

CONSEQUENCES OF ACCOUNT DELETION

Please note that account deletion is permanent and irreversible. After deletion:

- You will lose access to all consultation history
- You will no longer be able to access previous prescriptions through the app
- Any upcoming appointments will be cancelled
- You will need to create a new account to use JustKidding services again
